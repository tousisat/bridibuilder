My story

I used to be a very skinny guy back in 2013. Doing too much cardio and eating one time a day was my biggest mistake. I truly believed it was the way to go for a healthy lifestyle.

One day, one of my friend started to make fun of my body in front of other people by calling me names. I still remember how humiliated I felt and decided to take action. At first, I was hesitant in joining a gym as I felt people will make fun as well. But finally, with the help of a good friend, I joined the gym and started to lift with him.

I had my goal physique set in mind but without proper diet and training, I struggled with the results. After years of learning from my mistakes, I started to understand how my body respond and started to see noticible results.

I gained more then 50 pounds of mass over that 5 year transformation, became a certified personal trainer and now, I'm willing to help you guys achieve your goals.
